Common Questions

Everything You
Want to Know

Does Jessica sound like a real person or obviously like a bot?+
Jessica was trained specifically on luxury automotive sales conversations. She sounds warm, knowledgeable, and genuine — not robotic. She also complies with FTC requirements and will identify herself as an AI if a client directly asks. Most clients are surprised to learn she isn't human.
Can I review everything before it goes to clients?+
Yes — always. Every email Jessica drafts sits in a review queue. You read it, edit it if you want, and approve it before anything sends. Nothing goes to a client without your explicit sign-off. This is non-negotiable by design.
How long does it take to get set up?+
Most dealers are fully operational within one business day. We handle the setup, walk your team through the platform, and make sure Jessica is trained on your specific inventory and brand voice before you go live.
Does Pinnacle integrate with our existing DMS or lead sources?+
Pinnacle is designed to work alongside tools like Eleads, vAuto, and HomeNet. Lead intake from your website works immediately. Deeper DMS integrations are available on the Enterprise plan. We're adding direct integrations continuously.
Is Pinnacle compliant with FTC and TCPA regulations?+
Yes. Compliance is baked into the platform at every level — not added on top. AI disclosure, opt-in/opt-out management, messaging hour restrictions, CAN-SPAM compliance, and full audit trails are all included as standard. Our compliance framework was built with legal counsel.
What happens to our data? Who can see it?+
Your client data belongs to you. It is never shared with other dealers, never sold, and never used to train models without your consent. Each dealership's data is completely isolated. Full data export is available at any time.
